Welcome to The Third Turn Series Database, a simple way to list and query information about any racing series we have on our site. Over time, we will continue to look for new and informative ways to distribute information about the tours we proudly cover. For now, you will be able to access and search the database through the table on your right.

Key

  • (*) indicates the series is no longer active

Statistics & Misc. Info

  • We have information on over 1596 racing series and 714 special racing events on our site, as well as many other non-sanctioned races, qualifying races, and various other races.
  • The oldest active series we have on our site is the Badger Midget Auto Racing Association, which began their history in 1936 as the Wisconsin Racing Association.
  • The oldest single season we have on our site is the 1905 AAA Champ Car Series season. The first year of competition for the series. This is the series that would form the basis for the USAC Champ Car Series, and later the IndyCar Series.
  • The oldest active event we have on our site is the 1911 International 500. The inaugural running of what is now known as the Indianapolis 500.
  • The oldest race we have on our site is the 1894 Le Petit Journal Horseless Carriages Contest, which is considered to be the first organized automobile race ever contested.
